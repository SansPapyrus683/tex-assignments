                  \item From the get-go, we know $x$ takes the form of

                        It remains to figure out $a_1$ and $a_{-1}$.

                        Since $x(t)$ is even, we have
                        \[a_{-1}e^{-j\omega_0t}+4+a_1e^{j\omega_0t}=a_{-1}e^{j\omega_0t}+4+a_1e^{-j\omega_0t}\]
